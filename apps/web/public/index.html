How it works

A better alternative to generic activity playlists.

Spotify already offers playlists for activities, but those mixes are designed for broad audiences. Daily Dev Mix keeps the activity context while grounding recommendations in your own listening history, recent playback, and session behavior.

01

Start a real session

Choose the activity, start the session when you begin listening, and let the app capture the tracks and playback behavior tied to that moment.

02

Build a personal signal

Short sessions are supported with anchor tracks plus recent and top listening signals, so even a new activity profile starts from something personal.

03

Create the next playlist

End the session and Daily Dev Mix returns a playlist concept, rationale, and seed tracks shaped by the way you actually listened in that context.

🎯

Activity-aware capture

Keep study, workout, road trip, and unwind sessions separate so each mode builds its own taste profile over time.

⚓

Anchor tracks

Give a new activity a few unmistakable starting points before the session history is mature enough to stand on its own.

🧭

Discovery balance

Choose whether the next mix stays close to your habits or leans into something less familiar with a single slider.
